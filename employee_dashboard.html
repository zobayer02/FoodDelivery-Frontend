Delivery Drivers/Riders

Order Delivery: Pick up food orders from restaurants and deliver them to customers in a timely manner.

Route Navigation: Use GPS apps to find the most efficient routes for deliveries.

Customer Interaction: Ensure a positive customer experience during deliveries.

Order Verification: Verify that the correct order is picked up and delivered, ensuring quality.

Customer Service Representatives

Customer Support: Handle inquiries, complaints, and feedback from customers via phone, chat, or email.

Issue Resolution: Resolve issues related to orders such as delays or incorrect items.

Order Tracking: Provide customers with order status updates and estimated delivery times.

Restaurant Liaison/Account Managers

Restaurant Onboarding: Help onboard new restaurants to the platform.

Relationship Management: Maintain relationships with partner restaurants and address concerns.

Performance Monitoring: Monitor restaurant performance metrics to ensure service quality.

Operations Staff

Logistics Coordination: Coordinate delivery logistics, including scheduling and routes.

Inventory Management: Ensure delivery supplies are available as needed.

Process Improvement: Improve delivery processes and efficiency.

Marketing & Sales Staff

Promotional Campaigns: Develop and implement promotional campaigns to attract customers.

Market Research: Conduct market research to understand customer preferences and trends.

Community Engagement: Engage with the local community to promote the service.

IT & Technical Support

System Maintenance: Maintain the technology infrastructure of the platform.

Technical Support: Provide technical support to employees and customers.

Software Development: Work on enhancing the platform and improving user experience.

Quality Assurance

Service Quality Monitoring: Monitor service quality provided by drivers and restaurants.

Feedback Analysis: Analyze customer feedback and performance metrics for improvement.

Training and Standards: Help develop training programs and service standards.

Finance & Accounting

Financial Management: Manage budgeting, accounting, and payroll.

Transaction Oversight: Oversee payment processing, ensuring security and accuracy.

Reporting: Prepare financial reports to assess the health of the business.
